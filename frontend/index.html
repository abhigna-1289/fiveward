Practice Questions

Why I Built fiveward

I know what it's like to study hard for an AP exam with the wrong tools.

When I was preparing for my AP classes and exams, I spent hours jumping between websites, hunting for free resources, and finding workarounds for paywalls. Study guides that didn't match the College Board curriculum. Flashcard apps locked behind subscriptions. Hours wasted that could have been spent actually learning. And on top of all that, managing my time across everything I had going on made it even harder.

I did end up scoring well, but I kept thinking: none of that struggle was necessary. No student should have to waste their time searching for resources when they could be using that time to actually learn.

So I built it.

fiveward is everything I wished I had, completely free, fully aligned to the College Board curriculum, with study guides, flashcards, practice questions, and a built in study timer to help you manage your time, all in one place. No paywalls. No missing content. No compromises.

Every student deserves access to great study resources regardless of what they can afford. That's not just why I built fiveward, it's why I'll keep building it.

— Abhigna
